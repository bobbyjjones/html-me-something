Video Games

Growing up in the 80's and 90's, my formative years coincided with the rise of Nintendo. Needless to say, I was captivated by the electric allure of 8-Bit excitement. Some odd years later, I'm still mesmerized.

Nowadays, I have built a website that showcases and celebrates my adoration for retro-gaming: www.Retro-Grades.com! Please check it out!



Writing

I love to write. Essays, reviews, feature stories, short stories and lyrics... taking pen or pencil to paper is a marvelous way to fill the world with your thoughts, feelings and imagination.



Music Production

Music is soul-stirring. It speaks to me and conveys my moods. Ever since I can remember, I've always felt safe in its embrace. I played French horn and trumpet in school, and have crafted over 1,000 instrumental pieces with the help of my computer and trusty software. Music is always present and is so important to me.
